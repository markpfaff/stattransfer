About Deactivation


Your copy of Stat/Transfer allows you to install Stat/Transfer on three separate computers. Typically these will be a work computer, a home computer and a laptop.  

If you have used all three, you must deactivate one before activating on a fourth.  


To Deactivate Stat/Transfer:


1) On the "About" tab, click the Deactivate button.


2) A dialogue box will give an "Are you sure?" message.  Click Yes.


3) When the program confirms you are activated, close the program and proceed to activate on your new machine
