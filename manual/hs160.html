Starting Stat/Transfer
 		   

On Windows, the installation procedure will install a folder for Stat/Transfer in the Programs menu and a shortcut to Stat/Transfer.  A shortcut to Stat/Transfer will also be installed on your desktop. Click on either of the shortcuts to start the program.


On OS-X, click on  Stat/Transfer in the StatTransfer13 folder of your Applications folder.


On Linux, a shortcut to Stat/Transfer will be installed on your desktop.
